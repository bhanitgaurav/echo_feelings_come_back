# Privacy Policy for Echo

**Last Updated:** December 17, 2025

## 1. Introduction
Echo ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect
- **Phone Number**: Used for authentication and account creation.
- **Contacts**: We request access to your contacts to help you find friends on Echo. Contact data is hashed and matched securely; we do not store your raw contact list on our servers.
- **Biometric Data**: We use your device's biometric features (FaceID/TouchID) for local authentication. We do not store or access your biometric data; it remains on your device.
- **Messages ("Echoes")**: We store the messages you send and receive to provide the service. These are encrypted in transit and at rest.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and improve the Service.
- To authenticate your identity.
- To facilitate connections with other users.

## 4. Data Deletion
You can request deletion of your account and data by contacting us at support@echoapp.com or using the in-app "Delete Account" feature (coming soon).

## 5. Contact Us
If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at privacy@echoapp.com.
